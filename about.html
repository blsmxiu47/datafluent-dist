About Me

I am an aspiring data analyst with an interest in linguistics and natural language in general.

I enjoy learning for its own sake and I do these kinds of projects for fun in my free time. I hope to combine my passions for language and data to complete a formal study of computational linguistics in the future.

Why this Site?

Ultimately the purpose of the site is to supplement a résumé and work experience. I quite enjoy teaching ESL, but I’d really love to turn my hobbies into a career and break into data analytics.

It’s been a while since I’ve made any of my work available online, even on GitHub, so I figured I’d do some mini-projects in NLP with the express intent of explaining the steps, sort of like a post you’d see on Medium or an educational kernel on Kaggle. But then I realized it’d be much more fun to create my own website/portfolio from scratch as opposed to just adding comments to my existing code and uploading a kernel to some third-party site, so here we are.

I plan on including only recent projects of mine, so the quantity of projects will be low at first, increasing gradually as I diversify my skillset and explore new challenges. I link to my old analytics portfolio for more projects I have done, but keep in mind they'll be pretty outdated by the time you read this.
